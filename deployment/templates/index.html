Steps to use the website

Follow these steps to generate unique images:

  1. Click "Upload Image" and select a picture from your computer.
  2. In the "Prompts" field, enter descriptive keywords (comma-separated).
  3. In the "Patterns" field, enter pattern keywords (comma-separated).
  4. Click "Run Model" to generate an image based on your inputs.
  5. The result will be displayed on the right side.
  6. Download the result and explore different inputs!
